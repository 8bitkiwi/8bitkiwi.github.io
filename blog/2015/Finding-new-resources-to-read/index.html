Finding new resources to read

16 May 2015 , Taggged in: reading

An important part of my job as front-end developer is keeping up to date and staying close to new tools, trends, code demos and apps.

It’s hard to find high quality content as well as the content most relevant to me. And if you’re the kind of person like me who mostly reads about front-end and design its even harder.

Since there are many developers out there who are reading my blog I wanted share it here with you.

In a recent article, I introduced you to the “Newsletters for web designers and front-end developers”, which I firmly believe is a great way to get latest about web development right in your inbox. However, I prefer to read also on web when there are all read through.

Recently stumbled across the web and found Webdesigner News, they provide web designers and developers with a single location to discover the latest and most significant stories on the Web. Search through hundreds of posts on blogs, social media, and news channels, to deliver the most essential stories of the day. Their content covers quality news, fresh tools and apps, case studies, code demos, inspiration posts, videos and more.

And if you are too busy to check them daily check out their newsletter with the most shared stories of the day.
