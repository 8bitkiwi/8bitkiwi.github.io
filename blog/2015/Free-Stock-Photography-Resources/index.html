27 Feb 2015 , Taggged in: photography

Since I started coding, photography has always been an integral part of web design to me. There are websites that offers you a wide variety of options for finding stock photography. Not all of these stock photos are for free and some of them cost money. Before you use them in your design projects, always make sure you understand the restrictions on a particular image before you use it.
