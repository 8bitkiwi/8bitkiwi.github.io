A list of Front-end related conferences in Europe (2015)

22 Jan 2015 , Taggged in: conferences, front-end

This is a list of front-end related conferences. I thought it would be a great to share with everyone who’s into front-end development and UX. Here’s my list of favorites:

  • FITC Amsterdam - February 23-24, Amsterdam, Netherlands
  • Awwwards Conference - February 24-25, Barcelona, Spain
  • UX Riga - February 26, Riga, Latvia
  • jQuery UK - March 6, Oxford City, UK
  • .concat() - March 7, Salzburg, Austria
  • onGamestart - March 11-13, Warsaw, Poland
  • SmashingConf - March 17-18, Oxford, UK
  • Mobilism - March 27, Amsterdam, Netherlands
  • DotYork - April 9, York, UK
  • Bulgaria Web Summit - April 18, Sofia, Bulgaria
  • Naked.js - April 22, Norwich City, UK
  • Industry Conf - April 22, Newcastle upon Tyne, UK
  • Future of Web Design - April 27-29, London, UK
  • Front-Trends - May 6-8, Warsaw, Poland
  • Scotland JS - May 7-8, Edinburgh, Scotland
  • Beyond Tellerrand - May 11-13, Dusseldorf, Germany
  • UpFront - May 19, Manchester, UK
  • CSSDay - June 12, Amsterdam, Netherlands
  • AngularConnect - October 20 - 21, London
